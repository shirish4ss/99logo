Service Terms & Conditions

1. Payments & Pricing

  • 100% full payment is required upfront. No work begins without verification.
  • Payments are strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Prices are fixed: 99 INR (2 options), 150 INR (4 options), 200 INR (8 options).
  • "Rush Service" (5 hours) requires an additional 100 INR.
  • No discounts for bulk/future orders.

2. No-Mockup & RGB Policy (Strict)

  • Strictly No 3D Mockups: We provide flat 2D images only. No stationery or signage previews.
  • RGB Color Only: Files are optimized for digital screens. We do not provide CMYK (Print) profiles.
  • We do not guarantee color accuracy on physical printing machines.
  • We do not provide "Gold/Silver Foil" effects.

3. Timeline & Delivery

  • Standard delivery time is 48 working hours.
  • Working hours exclude Sundays and National Holidays.
  • The clock starts only after payment AND design brief are received.
  • We are not liable for delays caused by internet/technical outages.

4. Revisions & Final Adjustments

  • Strictly NO REVISIONS (starting over) are included.
  • Only two (2) small changes (color/font) are allowed on the final design.
  • Requesting a new icon is NOT a small change.
  • Small changes take 12 working hours.
  • Changing brand name mid-project requires a new payment.

5. Source Files & Deliverables

  • Source files provided ONLY for the final chosen logo.
  • Formats: PNG, EPS, AI, SVG, PDF, JPEG.
  • We do not provide source files for rejected options.
  • Files are sent in ZIP format. We do not teach how to use AI software.
  • File backup is kept for 7 days only. Retrieval fee: 50 INR.

6. Communication & Ownership

  • All communication must be written (Instagram DM/Email). No Voice/Video Calls.
  • Abusive behavior results in cancellation.
  • Copyright transfers to client upon final delivery. Rejected concepts remain our property.
  • We reserve the right to display the logo in our portfolio.
